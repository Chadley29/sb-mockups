Assisting Customers who fail authentication and are Overseas - Update

Good day Voice Branch,

Communication summary

This pertains to clients who are overseas and require the following assistance:

  1. Changing contact information
  2. Increasing the EAP limit
  3. Financial transfers

But has failed authentication
If the client passes authentication, please assist as per normal processes

This only applies if the client could not pass authentication and could not be referred to the branch because they are overseas. You can help the customer if you have successfully authenticated them.

We sent out communications about clients abroad on January 27, 2023. In the communication, we stated that customers calling from overseas should be sent either to their relationship banker, if they have one, or to the PSE team, if they do not. (To view the communication, click here.)

We have been made aware that this process is only applicable to the following demands ONLY.

  1. Changing contact information
  2. Increasing the EAP limit (PSE Team can increase the limit to just under a million rand)
  3. Financial transfers

If you receive a call from a customer who is currently overseas and needs assistance on the abovementioned demands, they will fall into one of these two categories:

  • A customer is overseas and they have a relationship manager, refer to the first attachment of this email
  • A customer is overseas and they do not have a relationship manager, refer to the second attachment of this email
You may also refer to the Easy Aid.
